# SBL Biblical Book Abbreviations

Use these abbreviations for Scripture references in site content and front matter. They follow the *SBL Handbook of Style*, second edition, sections 8.3.1–3. Book abbreviations do not take periods; use an en dash for inclusive ranges (for example, `Matt 11:25–30`).
