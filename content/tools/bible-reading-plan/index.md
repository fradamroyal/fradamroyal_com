Create a schedule for reading all 73 books of the Catholic Bible in their
canonical order. Choose an inclusive ending date or a total number of reading
days. The generator keeps chapters and the lettered additions to Esther intact,
then distributes them as evenly as possible.

The plan uses SBL book abbreviations and the chapter numbering of the New
American Bible Revised Edition. Esther’s Greek additions appear as A–F; Daniel
includes the longer chapter 3 and chapters 13–14. Everything is generated on
your device and no dates or plan data are sent anywhere.
